Privacy Policy for Scryfall NLP

Last updated: October 15, 2025

What We Collect

  • License keys — stored locally in your browser’s Chrome sync storage.
  • Search usage count — stored locally in your browser’s Chrome sync storage.

What We Don’t Collect

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Location data
  • Any personally identifiable information

How We Use Data

  • License keys are used to authenticate your access to the Scryfall NLP extension.
  • Your search queries are sent to our backend server only to convert natural language into valid Scryfall syntax.
  • We do not store, log, or share your search queries.

Where Data Is Stored

All locally stored data (license keys and usage counts) resides in your browser’s Chrome sync storage. We do not maintain external databases of user data.

API Usage

The extension uses a simple backend API to convert natural language into Scryfall syntax. Queries are processed temporarily in memory and are not logged or saved.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at scrysyntaxextension@gmail.com.
